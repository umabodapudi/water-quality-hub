Understand how to use this website

Water results depend on the sample, location, laboratory method, and type of water system.

Public water and private wells are different

Public water systems have regulatory monitoring requirements. Private well owners are generally responsible for testing and maintaining their wells.

Learn about private wells →

One result does not describe all water quality

A result usually represents one contaminant, one sample location, and one point in time. It does not prove that every use of the water is safe.
