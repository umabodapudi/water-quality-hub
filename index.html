PFAS / PFOS (Forever Chemicals):

Synthetic chemical compounds originating from industrial wastewater, water-repellent fabrics, and commercial firefighting foams. They stack up continuously in biological tissues and do not break down in nature.

Maximum Contaminant Level (MCL): 4.0 parts per trillion (ppt) for PFOA and PFOS parameters.

When to worry: If your city utility's quarterly public updates show a consistent trend running above 4.0 ppt over an entire year.
