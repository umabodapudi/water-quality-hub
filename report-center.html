Understand Terms in Your Water Report

Paste text from a Consumer Confidence Report or laboratory report. This tool identifies common drinking-water terms, abbreviations, units, measurements, and possible exceedance language.

This tool explains terminology such as MCL, MCLG, action level, violation, non-detect, and treatment technique. It does not analyze the entire report or determine whether your water is safe.

This tool provides general educational information. It does not replace guidance from your water utility, health department, or healthcare provider.
